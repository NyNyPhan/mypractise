TAIFUU Yosakoi Dance Team

TAIFUU Yosakoi dance team belongs to the Japanese club of Foreign Trade University.
The dance team gathers people who are passionate about dancing and love Japanese culture.
We always want to spread the joyful spirit and positive energy of Yosakoi dance to everyone

FJC_Foreign Trade Univesiry Japanese Club

FJC, the abbreviation of FTU Japanese Club, is the Japanese Club of Foreign Trade University Ho Chi Minh City.
FJC will bring you the most interesting knowledge about the culture and language of Japan, the country.

First step_Naruko is our friends

Naruko is one of the props of the yosakoi dance.
We have to learn how to use Naruko correctly and take care of it. We will be punished if we accidentally drop Naruko during practice or performance.
